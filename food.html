Tacos

Tacos are one of the most popular foods in Mexico. They can be filled with beef, pork, chicken, or vegetables.

Mole

Mole is a sauce made from chocolate, spices, and peppers. It is served during holidays and celebrations.

Enchiladas

Enchiladas are tortillas filled and rolled with cheese, meat, or beans, topped with chili sauce.

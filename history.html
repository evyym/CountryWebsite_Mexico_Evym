Ancient Mexico

Mexico was home to powerful civilizations like the Aztecs and Mayans. They built pyramids, calendars, and large cities.

The Pyramid of the Sun is one of the largest structures built by ancient Mexicans.

Spanish Arrival

Spain arrived in the 1500s and mixed European culture with Indigenous traditions.

Modern Mexico

Mexico gained independence in 1821 and has grown into a strong, creative, and diverse nation.
